Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 9, 2025

1. Information We Collect

When you use Bus2College, we collect:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, grade level
  • Student Profile: Academic information (GPA, test scores), contact details, parent/guardian information
  • Application Materials: College selections, essays, activities, recommender information
  • Usage Data: How you interact with the application

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and maintain the Bus2College service
  • Store and organize your college application materials
  • Provide AI-powered feedback and suggestions
  • Improve our services and user experience

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored using Supabase, a third-party database service. While we implement security measures, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. We recommend maintaining backups of important information.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with:

  • Service Providers: Supabase (database), OpenAI (AI features)
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law or to protect rights and safety

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request deletion of your account and data
  • Export your data
  • Opt-out of optional features (like AI feedback)

6. Cookies and Tracking

Bus2College uses browser local storage to maintain your session and save preferences. We do not use third-party advertising cookies.

7. Children's Privacy

Our service is intended for students aged 13+. Users under 18 should review this policy with a parent or guardian.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use of the service after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Contact Us

For privacy questions or to exercise your rights, contact us through the application or at https://bus2college.com.
