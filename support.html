Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account?

Click "Register" on the homepage and fill in your information. You'll need to provide your name, email, and create a password. Make sure to read and accept our Terms of Service.

Is Bus2College really free?

Yes! Bus2College is completely free to use. We're committed to making college application assistance accessible to all students.

How does the AI assistant work?

Our AI assistant uses advanced language models to help you with college essays, application questions, and guidance. It's trained to provide helpful, accurate information while respecting your privacy.

Is my data safe?

Absolutely. We use industry-standard encryption and secure databases (Supabase) to protect your information. Read our Privacy Policy for full details.

Can I use this on my phone?

Yes! Our website is mobile-friendly, and we're working on dedicated iOS and Android apps coming soon.

What if I forgot my password?

Click "Forgot Password?" on the login page. We'll send you a password reset link to your registered email address.

How do I delete my account?

Contact us at support@bus2college.com with your account deletion request. We'll process it within 48 hours and delete all your data as per our Privacy Policy.
